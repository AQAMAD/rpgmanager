Campaigns

A campaign represents all the events to unfold between the time you gather the player group and the time they have achieved a major goal.

You, as a GM, need to define this significant goal, and give the means to your PCs to achieve it.

To do so, the players will need to progress step-by-step towards this goal.

Each step on this way is represented by a Scenario (we will get there soon).

Think of your campaign as the "once-in-a-lifetime" accomplishment.

If you were designing the "Lord of the Rings" Campaign, this goal would be "destroy the One ring".

If you were designing the "Star Wars" Campaign, this goal would be "free the Galaxy".
