RPGM Concepts

As a GM, your experience with RPGM revolves around building Campaigns and using Ressources.

These concepts will be detailed to allow you to better organize your sessions and make full use of your creativity to build exciting adventures.

  • Campaigns are the major focus, they are the epic quests that you play for years.
  • Scenarios are their building blocks, the sequence campaigns and are the steps to victory, a game session is rarely enough to play out a scenario.
  • Sequences are scenarios's building blocks, they are the chapters in the book, individual events that your play, a typical game session takes your players through a few sequences.
  • Locations are important factors, they represent the places your player visit, or will visit throughout the campaign.
  • Actors are the major focus of interaction. Recurring villains, tribes of minions, sympathic NPCs and your player characters are all actors in your campaign.
  • Document Ressources are there to help you find your way through the huge reference of game books/texts you need, they help you get quickly to that nifty spell or the description of the 3rd level of the shinra complex.
  • Audio Ressources help you build that mood that you cherish so much.
